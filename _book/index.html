Community Partnerships

DESCRIPTION: Learn how to expand your programs, your resources, and your audience by partnering with people and organizations in the community.

LEARNING OBJECTIVES
 After completing the Community Partnerships module, you will be able to…
  • Describe how an effective community partnership can support connected learning (Section 1: Introduction)
  • Identify the benefits of a partnership to both the library and the partner (Section 2: What is a Community Partnership?)
  • Understand the opportunities and challenges involved in working with four different types of partners (Section 3: Types of Partners)
  • Develop and maintain a community partnership (Section 4: Establishing a Partner)
